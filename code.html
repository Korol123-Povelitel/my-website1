Our Story

Aperture & Slate was born from a simple observation: school memories deserve more than a static backdrop. We believe in the energy of the classroom, the intensity of the sports field, and the pride of the graduation stage.

Our team of visual storytellers brings a cinematic approach to every assignment. We don't just take pictures; we curate the visual legacy of your school's unique community.
